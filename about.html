01 : About me

Who am I?

I am a passionate cognitive scientist with a keen interest in developing serious games and therapy modules for cognitive training and productivity. With extensive experience in cognitive science and clinical psychology, I enjoy exploring innovative ways to enhance cognitive abilities and optimize human performance.

My Educational Background

I am a PhD in Cognitive Psychology from the Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. For my thesis, I tried to explore the attentional control network and emotion perception in vulnerability to depression using behavioral techniques.

My future projects

For my future research endeavours, I aim to gain experience in incorporating computational neuroscience approaches to explore the underlying mechanisms of human cognition. I am also interested more broadly in designing psychotherapeutic interventions for clinical population
